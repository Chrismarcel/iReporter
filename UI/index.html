Make your voice count

iReporter is a platform that enables citizens report cases of corruption to appropriate authorities and also report issues that need government intervention. As a user of the platform, you can also view reports and interventions created by other users.

Create Report View Reports

How it works

In 3 easy steps you can easily report corruption cases and seek government intervention.

1

Create and submit a red flag or intervention report. Set a title, attach images/videos and input your location.

2

Your report gets submitted to the appropriate authority and process is put in place start reviewing your report.

3

Your report gets reviewed and you get a real time email feedback upon completion of the reviews.
